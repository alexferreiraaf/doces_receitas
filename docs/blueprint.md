# **App Name**: Doce Estimativa

## Core Features:

- Ingredient Inventory: Create, edit, and delete ingredients, including name, quantity, unit of measure, and price.
- Recipe Creation: Create, edit, and delete recipes, including name, ingredients, and instructions.
- Cost Calculation: Automatically calculate the cost of a recipe based on the cost of its ingredients.
- Profit Margin Calculation: Calculate the profit margin for a recipe based on the cost of production and the desired selling price.
- Recipe Generation (AI): AI Tool helps you suggest new recipes using your inventory ingredients. Simply describe what type of recipe you want to make (e.g., a cake, a cookie, a pie, etc.) and the AI will provide suggestions for recipes you can make, and a complete list of the ingredients to use with amounts. It can help suggest quantities that go well together, but these can be modified later
- Recipe Sharing: Share recipes with other users.
- Local Storage Persistence: Recipes and ingredients will be stored in the user's local storage

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep pink (#D81B60), evoking a sense of warmth, creativity, and sophistication appropriate for a cooking application.
- Background color: Very light pink (#FCE4EC), to match the overall branding, for a soft, inviting feel.
- Accent color: Purple (#8E24AA) for highlights, buttons, and important UI elements, offering contrast and drawing attention.
- Body font: 'Inter', a versatile sans-serif font for a clean, modern, readable interface.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a stylish sans-serif font that works well for headlines.
- Use simple, outline-style icons related to cooking and baking.
- Subtle animations for transitions and interactions, such as recipe loading and saving.
